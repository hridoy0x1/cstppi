About Our Department

The Department of Computer Science and Technology at Pabna Polytechnic Institute is a premier center for technical education, dedicated to producing skilled professionals in the field of computing and information technology.

Established with a vision to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, our department offers comprehensive diploma programs that prepare students for the challenges of the modern tech industry.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and industry partnerships, we ensure our students receive hands-on training in programming, networking, database management, web development, and emerging technologies.
